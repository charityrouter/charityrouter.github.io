Our
Services

Charmp! makes donations easy

Charmp! is a Charity Mobile Application which creates an efficient platform connects charity groups with interested donors.

Transparent money flow

Do you ever wonder where your donation dollars go? By Charmp!, Donors can get their donation details from monthly reports. This report describes how the charity groups utilize your donation in the projects.

Worry-free recurring donation

After choosing your donated preferences and the credit card setting, you are on the way to spread loves every month - NON STOP

World map

Charmp! connects with charity groups worldwide. Tracking your donation by the our fun world map and share your achievement.

Trusty Charity Organizations

Charmp! collaborates with high quality charity groups, we are committed to making your donation more meaningful.

Share with friends

You can easily share your achievement by social media, encouraging your friends, family to join and to do something good today!

Multi-Media feedback & Real-time report

Every 2 weeks, you will receive a thank-you message from the projects you supported. The message would be a video or audio.
